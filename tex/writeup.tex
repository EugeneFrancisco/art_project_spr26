\section*{Embeddings as Price Predictors}
For each artist $a$, let $x_a$ be its associated embedding vector. Let $y_a$ be the median log price for $a$. We train a regressor $\hat f_\theta(\cdot)$ to predict median log prices by minimizing the squared loss
\begin{align*}
	L(\theta; X) = \sum_{a\in A}\left(\hat f_\theta(x_a) - y_a\right)^2.
\end{align*}
